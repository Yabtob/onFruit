Our Story

The idea for Onfruit was born in a local market, where we noticed how many small fruit farmers struggle to reach modern consumers . Yeabsira, a computer science student, teamed up with friends to create a platform that connects local growers directly to urban buyers . Inspired by seeing food waste and pricing issues firsthand, we wanted to bring transparency and tech to the fresh food industry. Vision:

We imagine a future where every fruit we eat supports a local farmer and strengthens a sustainable food chain.

Mission:

To empower local fruit growers through technology, and deliver healthy, fresh produce directly to communities.
